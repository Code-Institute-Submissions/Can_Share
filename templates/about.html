About:

Can share was spawned out of a love for beer with good friends. Once a month we would meet up at our local brewery and each bring a can of something new to sample. We'd discuss our opinions on taste, smell, mouth feel/ body, colour, can or bottle design and how the can/bottle feels in the touch. Is it a quality beer in a shoddy can or a shoddy beer in a quality can, or a good all-rounder? After trying a few we would do an overall rating of the beer we had sampled!

To keep track of our best and worst sampled beers, I decided to create can share, a website for all beer lovers to come and give a thumbs up or thumbs down to different beers.

The Original Team!
